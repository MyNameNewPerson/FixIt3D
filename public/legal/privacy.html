Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services. This includes search queries, preferred language, and non-precise geolocation (if permitted) to show nearby 3D printing masters.

We use local storage and cookies to remember your display preferences (Spare Parts vs. Hobby mode) and language settings.
